## Long description

World Models Atlas is a bilingual research portal focused on world models and adjacent areas like embodied AI, robotics, and physical AI. It organizes the space into curated paper pages, project pages, timeline milestones, topic hubs, and weekly update streams so readers can understand both foundational work and the newest progress. The goal is to give researchers, builders, and AI-focused operators a single entry point instead of forcing them to track scattered papers, demos, blogs, and social posts one by one.
